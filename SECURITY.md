## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you identify a security vulnerability, please follow the steps below to report it:

Contact Us: Reach out to our team using the contact information provided below.

Acknowledgment: You will receive an acknowledgment of your report within 48 hours.

Assessment: The vulnerability will be assessed to determine its impact and severity.

Resolution Timeline: Updates regarding the resolution will be provided periodically, typically every 5-7 business days.

Final Decision: You will be informed whether the vulnerability is accepted or declined, along with reasoning in the latter case.

Your cooperation helps us ensure a secure and robust environment for all users.
